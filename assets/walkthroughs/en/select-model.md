## Use models in Copilot Chat

Once a provider is configured, its models are available in Copilot Chat.

---

### Open Copilot Chat

Press `Ctrl+Alt+I` (macOS: `Cmd+Alt+I`) or click the Copilot icon in the Activity Bar.

### Select a model

Click the model name at the top of the chat panel to open the model picker. DeepSeek and MiniMax models appear listed under their provider names.

---

### Models not showing up?

Language models may be hidden by default. Open the Language Models panel and make sure the providers are enabled.

[Open Language Models →](command:workbench.action.chat.manage)
